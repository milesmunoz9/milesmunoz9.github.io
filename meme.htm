MEME

For this assignment, we were tasked with combining two or more memes into one. I chose to use the "thousand yard stare" meme with the staring Hank meme, deriving from a the Breaking Bad character. By combining the two memes, I wanted to maintain the original emotion/feeling of the original memes, and create a seamless mixture.
